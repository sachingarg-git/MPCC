🚫 6. De-registration Management Flow

📍 Click: De-registration from CRM sub-menu

1
🖱️ Click "De-registration" in sub-menu
→ Opens De-registration page with status buckets
2
🖱️ Click any status bucket card
→ Filters table by: Pending | Under Review | Approved | Rejected | Completed
3
🖱️ Click "Review" button on row
→ Opens detail modal with checklist and history tabs

1

→ Opens renewal form modal
2
📝 Fill renewal details
